Tutoring & Programs

Orange County, CA & Remote Worldwide

Our academy offers a variety of specialized programs to help students learn and grow in the field of computer science and programming.

1-on-1 Tutoring

Tailored 1-on-1 sessions. From kids learning the basics to college-level coursework, we create those "Aha!" moments.

Career guidance and long-term planning for students pursuing CS in college.
